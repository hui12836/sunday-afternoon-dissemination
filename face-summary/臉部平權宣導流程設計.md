### 🌸 Face Equality Introduction

**Point to the first poster:**
> "Today we want to share an idea called **'Face Equality.'** Look at this scale — on one side is appearance, on the other side is skills and experience. We believe that a person's value comes from what they contribute, not how they look. Please give people a fair chance."

**Point to the second poster:**
> "Especially for friends who have burns or look different — do you know what they want the most?"

*(Wait for them to guess)*

> "They say — **'Just give us a smile, and we are happy!'** They don't need extra sympathy. They just want to be treated normally."

**Introduce the three ideas:**
> "Here are three simple things we can all do:

> First, **focus on ability** — see what people can do, not what they look like.

> Second, **treat them as equals** — believe they are strong enough, and give them the same respect as anyone else.

> Third, **act natural** — stay relaxed, don't stare, and don't avoid them."
